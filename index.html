/ About Based in Berlin, Germany

Curious
by training,
deliberate by habit.

I didn't start in design — I started in curiosity. A communications degree, a first UX role at an agency, and a slow realisation that the questions I loved asking (why do people do what they do? what makes a product feel effortless?) were exactly the questions good design answers.

Five years in, I've designed end-to-end across B2B logistics, consumer trust, and internal tooling. I'm a generalist by choice — with a T-shaped spine in research and growth. I've run the first focus group in a 25-year-old company, introduced heatmaps that reshaped a brand, and turned a price set on intuition into a 15% revenue lift.

Breadth is the point. The work that excites me is the full loop — from a fuzzy strategic question to shipped UI to measured outcome, with a design system, a research habit, and a sharper team left behind afterwards.

Outside of product work I teach , sketch weekly, and read more behavioural science than is strictly healthy.

The shape of the work

Broad across the craft.
Deep where it moves the needle.

Most problems don't sit neatly inside one discipline. I've made it my job to be fluent across the stack — and to go deep in the two places where depth compounds: research and growth. Below, literally.

Controller

Server logs (GitHub Pages)

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub Inc., USA). When you open it, your browser transmits technically necessary data: IP address, date and time, requested URL, referrer, browser, and operating system. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in stable operation). Details: GitHub Privacy Statement.

Google Fonts

Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts (Google Ireland Ltd.). In the process, your IP address is transmitted to Google. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. Details: Google Privacy Policy.

YouTube embeds

Some case studies embed videos from YouTube (Google Ireland Ltd.). I use the privacy-enhanced mode (youtube-nocookie.com), which means YouTube does not set cookies until you press play. Once you start a video, your IP address and interaction data are transmitted to YouTube and Google. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent through clicking play) and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. Details: Google Privacy Policy.

Local storage

The browser's localStorage remembers your design preferences (variation, accent colour, type scale). This data stays on your device, is never transmitted, and can be cleared from your browser settings at any time.

Getting in touch

If you email me, I process the information you send in order to respond to your enquiry. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) or (f) GDPR. Emails are deleted once the purpose no longer applies.

Your rights

You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, and objection at any time (Art. 15 - 21 GDPR). A short email is enough.
